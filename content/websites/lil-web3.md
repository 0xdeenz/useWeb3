lil web3 aims to build really simple, intentionally-limited versions of web3 protocols & apps. By distilling them to their roots, we can better understand how they work.

- lil ENS
- lil OpenSea
- lil Fractional
- lil JuiceBox
- lil Flash Loan
- lil Gnosis
- lil Superfluid
